sparkling water Perrier Sparkling Natural Mineral Water, the source for which dates back over 100 million years, became part of the Nestle Waters North America family of brands in 1992. Perrier Sparkling Natural Mineral Water comes from a source in the south of France. Located near the small village of Vergeze, in Provence, the spring’s unique balance of minerals and addition of light effervescence provides its distinctively fresh, clean taste. It is this singular blend of gases and minerals which has made Perrier the world’s most requested sparkling natural water.
